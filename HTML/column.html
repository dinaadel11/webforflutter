Column

Column Widget in Flutter In Flutter, the Column widget is a fundamental layout widget that arranges its child widgets vertically, one below the other. It's a versatile tool for creating structured and organized layouts in your Flutter applications. Key Properties of Column Widget:

  • mainAxisAlignment: Controls how the children are distributed along the main axis (vertical).
  • MainAxisAlignment.start: Aligns children to the start of the main axis.
  • MainAxisAlignment.end: Aligns children to the end of the main axis.
  • MainAxisAlignment.center: Centers the children along the main axis.
  • MainAxisAlignment.spaceAround: Distributes space evenly around the children.
  • MainAxisAlignment.spaceBetween: Distributes space evenly between the children.
  • MainAxisAlignment.spaceEvenly: Distributes space evenly between the children, including the spaces at the beginning and end.
  • crossAxisAlignment: Controls how the children are aligned along the cross axis (horizontal).
  • CrossAxisAlignment.start: Aligns children to the start of the cross axis.
  • CrossAxisAlignment.end: Aligns children to the end of the cross axis.
  • CrossAxisAlignment.center: Centers the children along the cross axis.
  • CrossAxisAlignment.stretch: Stretches the children to fill the available width.
